ABOUT ME

Born in '96 a small town near Lucca, Italy with music in my ears when I was still in my mom's belly, raised - in what's probably the smallest bedroom ever - with totally differend kinds of music from the 80s to late 90s/early '00s, I've always found something interesting in every piece of music - apart from reggaeton.
I've been playing guitar since 2010 with a very slow learning curve that had just improved recently by taking lessons again. In that many years I became an overly-excited-nerdy guy on guitars, tone and anything I can spend money on.
Started singing just since 2021, I've really improved in this small timeframe - you can hear me giving my best shot in the covers I've posted around here, but please don't judge me too hard.
Sometimes you can even find me working, but not in a competitive way.
If you're looking for a mixing engineer, a guitarist, a nerd or a complete stupid you're in the right place. Scroll down and get to know me a little bit more!

I've attended a mentoring one-on-one mixing course with Jason "Jay" Maas, now mixing engineer and former Defeater guitarist, which taught me lots of things and introduced me to another way to spend my entire salary. I really thank him for the time he spent with me and the work we did together, as it really made me a better mixing engineer and I'm able to call myself so just because of him.
Mostly active on SoundCloud, if you want to hear my works you can go on my main account, where I post originals and covers recorded and mixed by me, or on my mixing account to hear some samples on how I work.
I've been mostly mixing my things and some songs from the URM Academy's Nail The Mix - but I'm always up to work with anyone, so if you're interesed feel free to drop me a message! Let's work together :)

PHOTOS

Taking pictures is a thing I've discovered to love in the years, going from a simple kit reflex to a fullframe and some analog gear. I took the opposite direction with analog-digital shooting, still not sure why.
I own a beautiful Sony a7c that I bring with me every time I get the chance to take pictures, a smaller but super fun Panasonic Lumix LX-100 that combines portability and picture quality, a point-and-shoot Olympus Mju-I running on film and my newest toy to the collection: a Minolta Dynax 7000i to reuse my old A-Mount lenses.
In the carousel in this section there are some of my favorite pictures taken, you can find more (and in higher quality) on my Flickr profile.
