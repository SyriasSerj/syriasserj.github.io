I've been playing guitar since 2010 and, while not being the best at it, I occasionaly get some results that make me happy. I've only started singing recently, around October 2021, but I'm feeling quite confident. Not too much though.
My artist name is bluehole. and you can find me on Soundcloud and Spotify. I've discovered to be a tone enthusiast during the years, but only from amp sims as I really don't have any space in my house (my neighbours are also very happy).
Currently my rig is:

  • Fender Jaguar American 60s - my favorite guitar, my go-to whenever I wanna randomly play or try to write something new. Its sound is fantastic and everything is stock, from tuning keys to pickups - apart from the bridge which is a StayTrem 9,5" bridge and I love it.
  • PRS SE Custom 24 - with an unique colorway called "Poplar Burl Green" (I think) that's my second favorite. I mostly use that for metal, but with splitcoils you can do pretty much anything and that's the very best part of it!
  • Jackson HT7 Pro - the Misha Mansoor cheap signature, just to get that djenty 0000s I really need in my life. Used mostly for modern metal, I really dig it as it's light and sounds great for a price that doesn't break the bank. Not a shredder tho, only syncopated riffs and 0s.
  • SubZero Rogue VI - heavily influenced by Loathe, I bought it as it's a cheaper Fender Bass VI but with humbuckers and it honestly sounds nice! Not the best axe but definitely a good and fun one.
